> [!quote] Sum Rule
> - The Sum Rule Says:
> 	- The derivative of $f + g = f' + g'$
> - In other words, we can work out each derivative separately and add them 

___
## Example
- Using the Power Rule:
$$\dfrac {d}{dx}x^2 = 2x$$
$$\dfrac{d}{dx}x^3 = 3x^2$$
- And so:
	- The derivative of $\large x^2+x^3 = 2x + 3x^2$
