>[!def] Definition
>$T_pM \to \text{tangent space}$
>$T_p^*M \to \text{dual space}$
>>[!def] Example:
>>$$\text{Let} \; v_1 = \begin{pmatrix}1\\2\\3\end{pmatrix} \in T_pM$$
>>- If $v' \in T_p^*M$
>>- Then: $v' = (v_1)^T = (v_1^*)^T$
>>	- $v' = (1\;2 \; 3)$
>>	- $v_1 v' = 1^2 + 2^2 + 3^2 = \text{Real Number}$
