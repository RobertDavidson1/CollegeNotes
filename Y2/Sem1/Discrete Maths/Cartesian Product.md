>[!def] The Cartesian Product
>- Given two sets $A,B$
>$$A \times B = \set{(x,y) : x \in A\; \text{and} \; y \in  B}$$

___
>[!example] Example
>- Let $A = \set{1,2,3}, B = \set{1,3,5}$ and $C = \set{2,4}$
>___
>1. 
>$$ A \times B = \{(1, 1), (1, 3), (1, 5), (2, 1), (2, 3), (2, 5), (3, 1), (3, 3), (3, 5)\} $$
>2.
>$$ A \times C = \{(1, 2), (1, 4), (2, 2), (2, 4), (3, 2), (3, 4)\} $$
