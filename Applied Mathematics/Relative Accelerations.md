>[!eq] $\\$
>- Let the accelerations of $A$ and $B$ relative to $O$ be:
>	- $a_{A}= \dfrac{dv_A}{dt}$
>	- $a_{B}= \dfrac{dv_B}{dt}$
>- We then find the **Relative Accelerations:**
>$$a_{B|A} = \dfrac{dv_{B|A}}{dt} = \text{Acceleration of B from A}$$ 
>$$\large = \dfrac{d}{dt}(v_{B}-v_{A}) = a_{B}-a_{A}$$
