**Refractive index:** ratio of speed of light in a vacuum to speed of light in material
$$n = \dfrac{c}{v}$$

**Wavelength from one meidum to another:**
$$\lambda' = \dfrac{\lambda}{n}\quad\begin{align*}&\lambda':\text{Wavelength in new medium}\\&\lambda:\text{Wavelegnth of light in air}\\&n:\text{Refractive index of new medium}\end{align*}$$

**Snell's Law:**
$$n_1\sin\theta_1 = n_2\sin\theta_2$$

**Real vs Apparent Depth:**
$$n_1 = n_2\dfrac{\text{Real}}{\text{Apparent}}$$

**Total Internal Reflection:**
$$\sin\theta_c = \dfrac{n_2}{n_1}\quad\begin{align*}&\theta_c:\text{Critical angle}\\&n_1:\text{first medium (higher index)}\\&n_2:\text{second miedum (low index)}\\\end{align*}$$
